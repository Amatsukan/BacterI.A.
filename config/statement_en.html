The Goal

Conquer the Petri dish! Expand your bacterial colony, harvest nutrients, and eliminate your opponent's cells before the turn limit runs out.

 Rules

The Map

A 64 × 64 square grid. Player 0 starts with 1 cell at (0, 0) (top-left) and Player 1 at (63, 63) (bottom-right).

Nutrient Spots are scattered symmetrically on the grid (180° rotational symmetry). Each spot has a type:

TypeCodeTotal Energy
Small110
Medium230
Large370

If one of your cells sits on a nutrient spot, it automatically extracts 1 energy per turn until the spot is depleted.

Energy

Starting energy is calculated as the distance from your base to the nearest nutrient, plus a safety margin. Energy pays for actions.

Actions (up to 5 per turn)

ActionSyntaxCostEffect
EXPANDEXPAND x y2 Create a new cell at (x,y). Must be empty and adjacent (orthogonal or diagonal) to one of your cells.
ATTACKATTACK x y2 Attack an enemy cell at (x,y). Succeeds if you have strictly more allied neighbors around the target than the defender. On success: enemy cell dies, you place your cell there, and gain +3 energy. On failure: energy is still spent.
AUTOPHAGYAUTOPHAGY x y0 Sacrifice one of your own cells at (x,y). Recovers +1 energy.
WAITWAIT0 Do nothing.

Separate multiple actions with ;. Example: EXPAND 1 0; EXPAND 0 1; WAIT

Fog of War

You always see all nutrient spots on your own half of the map. Enemy cells and enemy-half spots are only visible if they are within Chebyshev distance 3 of any of your cells.

Victory

  • Knockout: Destroy all of the opponent's cells.
  • Turn limit (500 turns): The player with the highest score wins. Score = cells × 100 + energy. Tiebreaker: total enemy cells destroyed.

 Expert Rules

  • Both players' actions are collected simultaneously, then resolved in player index order (Player 0 first). Within one player's actions, they are resolved left-to-right.
  • If both players try to EXPAND into the same empty cell in the same turn, the first player to be resolved wins.
  • A failed EXPAND (occupied or non-adjacent) still costs energy.
  • A failed ATTACK (no majority) still costs energy.
  • A failed AUTOPHAGY (not your cell) does not cost/gain energy.
  • Passive nutrient extraction happens after all actions, once per turn.
  • The map is symmetrical by 180° rotation. Nutrient spots are guaranteed reachable from both starting positions.

 Game Protocol

Initialization input
Line 1: two integers mapSize myIndex — grid size (always 64) and your player index (0 or 1).
Line 2: spotCount — number of nutrient spots on your half of the map.
Next spotCount lines: x y type — position and type code (1=Small, 2=Medium, 3=Large).
Input per turn
Line 1: myEnergy oppEnergy
Line 2: entityCount
Next entityCount lines, one of:
  • MYCELL x y
  • OPPCELL x y
  • SPOT x y type remainingEnergy
Output
A single line with up to 5 actions separated by ;.
Example: EXPAND 1 0; ATTACK 3 4; WAIT
Constraints
Grid size = 64
Max turns = 500
Response time per turn ≤ 50 ms
First turn response time ≤ 1000 ms

Turn 1 input (Player 0):

15 20
2
