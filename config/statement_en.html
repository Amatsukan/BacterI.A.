The Goal

Conquer the Petri dish. Expand your bacterial colony, harvest nutrients and eliminate your opponent's cells before the turn limit runs out.

 Rules

Two players start at opposite corners of a 64×64 grid, each with 1 cell and 20 energy.

Actions (up to 5 per turn, separated by ;)

  • EXPAND x y — Cost: 2 energy. Creates a new cell adjacent (orthogonal or diagonal) to one of your existing cells.
  • ATTACK x y — Cost: 2 energy. Attack an adjacent enemy cell. Succeeds if you have more allied neighbors around the target than the enemy does. Reward: +3 energy.
  • AUTOPHAGY x y — Cost: 0. Sacrifice one of your own cells, recovering 1 energy.
  • WAIT — Do nothing.

Victory Conditions

  • Knockout: Eliminate all enemy cells.
  • Turn limit: After 200 turns, the player with the most cells wins. Tiebreaker: banked energy / 10. Second tiebreaker: cells destroyed.

Fog of War

You only see enemies and neutral spots within a 3-cell radius of your cells. Your own half of the map is fully visible.

 Game Input / Output

Initialization input (once)
Line 1: mapSize — size of the square grid (always 64).
Input per turn
Line 1: myEnergy oppEnergy
Line 2: visibleEntitiesCount
Next visibleEntitiesCount lines: entity data (to be defined).
Output per turn
A single line with up to 5 actions separated by ;.
Example: EXPAND 1 0; WAIT
Constraints
Response time per turn ≤ 50 ms (first turn ≤ 1000 ms).
